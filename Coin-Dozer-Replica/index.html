Coin Dozer Replica! (Beta)

By PixelHyperCube!

A miniproject made using Matter.js and p5.js!

View this project on Github!
Apologies! - it works best on computer at the moment, will attempt to make it easier on other platforms soon!

Inspired by the original Coin Dozer!

(Used to play this game as a kid!)

Link to game!

How to play:

  1. Drop coins onto the platform to push the coins into the collector!
  2. Be careful, coins will be lost when they fall on the sides!
  3. How long will you last with a 100 coins in your pocket?

Value of coins

Coin Worth
Diamond Coin 10 Coins
Silver Coin 2 Coins
Normal Coin 1 Coin
